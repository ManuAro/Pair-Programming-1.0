## FAQ SECTION

### For Companies

**Q: How is this different from Checkr or Sterling?**
A: They verify for one employer. We create a portable passport the contractor can reuse. You get instant verification instead of waiting 3-6 weeks.

**Q: What if the background check finds issues?**
A: Provisional approval is revoked immediately. You're protected with our fraud guarantee.

**Q: How long is a passport valid?**
A: 12 months. Contractors can renew annually to maintain active status.

**Q: Can I trust the verification?**
A: Every passport is cryptographically signed and tamper-proof. Our public verification API lets you validate authenticity in real-time.

**Q: What regulations does this cover?**
A: We meet KYC/AML requirements for fintech, HIPAA for healthtech, and GDPR/CCPA for privacy. Background checks are performed by accredited providers.

### For Contractors

**Q: How much does a passport cost for me?**
A: FREE if your employer requests verification through our platform. $99 for self-service verification.

**Q: Who owns my data?**
A: YOU do. You control who sees your passport and can revoke access anytime.

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: 7-10 business days for full clearance. Employers can give provisional approval in 24h.

**Q: What gets verified?**
A: Identity, criminal background, employment history (2 most recent), education, technical credentials (GitHub/LinkedIn), and 2 professional references.
