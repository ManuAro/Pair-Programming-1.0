# Contractor Outreach Templates

**Objective:** Get 10 real contractors to test the beta
**Success metric:** At least 3 complete the OAuth verification flow

---

## Target Audience

**Ideal beta testers:**
1. **Freelance developers** on LinkedIn/Twitter who:
   - Recently posted about client work
   - Have active GitHub profiles
   - Mention "contractor" or "freelance" in bio

2. **Agency contractors** who:
   - Work with multiple clients
   - Have compliance/verification pain points
   - Are in regulated industries (finance, healthcare)

3. **International contractors** who:
   - Work with US clients
   - Face extra verification hurdles
   - Mention visa/remote work challenges

## When to Pivot

**Red flags that demand doesn't exist:**

1. **<10% reply rate to DMs**
   - **Interpretation:** Message isn't resonating
   - **Action:** Try Template 2 (Twitter curiosity hook)

2. **People sign up but don't complete OAuth**
   - **Interpretation:** OAuth flow is too complicated or not valuable enough
   - **Action:** Simplify onboarding or add clearer value explanation

3. **People complete OAuth but say "this isn't useful"**
   - **Interpretation:** Wrong problem or wrong solution
   - **Action:** Deep dive interview - what would make this useful?

4. **Everyone says "I'd use this if it was free"**
   - **Interpretation:** Not a strong enough pain point to pay for
   - **Action:** Pivot to different problem or add more value

**Green lights that demand exists:**

1. **>20% reply rate to DMs**
2. **>50% complete OAuth after signing up**
3. **Users ask "when is background check integration ready?"**
4. **Users refer other contractors unprompted**
5. **Users ask about pricing before trying**

## Pro Tips

1. **Personalize the first sentence** - shows you're not spamming
2. **Keep it under 100 words** - respect their time
3. **One clear call-to-action** - don't give them 3 options
4. **Send Monday-Wednesday** - higher response rates than Thu-Fri
5. **Follow up once, then stop** - don't be annoying
6. **Offer incentive for feedback** - "$25 gift card for 15 min call"
7. **Be transparent about beta** - don't oversell
8. **Screenshot bugs they report** - shows you care
9. **Thank everyone who tries it** - even if feedback is negative
10. **Ship fast based on feedback** - show you're listening

## Emergency Pivots

**If after 10 DMs nobody cares:**

**Option A: Different Audience**
- Try agencies instead of individual contractors
- Try HR departments who hire contractors
- Try staffing firms who verify contractors

**Option B: Different Problem**
- Instead of "speed up verification", pitch "portable reputation"
- Instead of targeting contractors, target employers
- Instead of B2B2C (us ‚Üí contractor ‚Üí employer), go B2B (us ‚Üí employer)

**Option C: Different Solution**
- Instead of provisional credentials, do "verification pre-approval"
- Instead of 24h credentials, do "same-day background checks"
- Instead of OAuth signals, do "reference network graph"
